## Featured Projects

### NagarFlow: AI-Powered Civic Intelligence Platform

Predictive civic resource platform for Mumbai that forecasts urban demand 48 hours in advance and dispatches water tankers and garbage trucks across 65+ zones, powered by 51,440+ real municipal complaint records and a 25+ endpoint Flask API.

- Multilingual NLU pipeline in Hindi, English, and Marathi that ingests complaints from WhatsApp, voice calls, and web chat
- Equity engine that amplifies under-reporting low-income wards so silent communities are served proportionally
- Closed-loop ML feedback system that fires automatic model retraining alerts when prediction error exceeds 25%
- Addresses the Rs 1,500+ crore Indian municipalities spend annually on reactive, complaint-driven resource allocation

**Tech:** `Python` `Flask` `Next.js` `Gemini 2.5 Pro` `Sarvam AI` `n8n` `SQLite`

[Repository](https://github.com/vinitgirdhar/nagarflow) | [Live Demo](https://nagarflow.netlify.app)

---

### Nirogya: Smart Public Health Surveillance Platform

**Smart India Hackathon 2025 Grand Finalist**

Disease surveillance platform for rural and tribal communities that monitors waterborne diseases through real-time outbreak tracking, water quality monitoring, and community reporting.

- Multilingual, mobile-first reporting built for low-connectivity communities to flag symptoms early
- Enables health officials to detect and contain outbreaks before they spread
- Selected as a Grand Finalist at Smart India Hackathon 2025, India's largest national innovation competition

**Tech:** `Machine Learning` `Real-Time Outbreak Tracking` `Multilingual Support` `Mobile-First`

---

### GRID: ML-Based Taxi Demand Forecasting Platform (New York City)

AI-based demand forecasting platform that predicts taxi demand 1 to 24 hours in advance using historical trip patterns, weather, public holidays, and event data.

- Processed and analyzed 100,000+ trip records to identify spatial and temporal demand trends across multiple city zones, integrating 5+ external data sources
- Demand heatmaps and hourly forecasts guide drivers to high-demand locations before demand peaks
- Reduces driver idle time by 20 to 35% and increases estimated daily driver revenue by 15 to 25%

**Tech:** `Machine Learning` `Time Series Forecasting` `Geospatial Analytics` `Python`

[Repository](https://github.com/vinitgirdhar/GRID_)

---

### VoiceQual: AI Voice Lead Qualification Platform

Full-stack AI sales intelligence platform where a conversational voice agent autonomously makes initial qualification calls and LLaMA 3 scores every conversation from 0 to 10 on the BANT framework (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline).

- Targets the 60 to 70% of rep time typically lost on leads that never convert
- Auto-surfaces only high-intent prospects so sales teams talk exclusively to people ready to buy

**Tech:** `ElevenLabs Conversational AI` `Groq LLaMA 3` `Full-Stack`

---

### Ledgerly: AI-Powered Personal Finance Platform

OCR and AI pipeline that automatically extracts transaction details from receipts and bills, categorizes expenses, and surfaces actionable spending insights.

- Eliminates manual data entry from personal finance tracking
- AI-driven expense categorization enabling smarter money decisions

**Tech:** `OCR` `AI Expense Categorization` `Python`

[Repository](https://github.com/vinitgirdhar/Ledgery)

---

### Sahaayak: E-Commerce Marketplace Connecting Street Vendors to Wholesalers

E-commerce platform that directly connects local street food vendors, such as vada pav and bhel puri sellers who lack the volume for large-scale orders, with wholesalers, giving micro-vendors access to wholesale supply chains without middlemen.

- End-to-end commerce flows covering vendor discovery, cart and checkout, and order tracking
- Wholesaler product management, profile management, and analytics for both sides of the marketplace

**Tech:** `Flask` `Python` `Full-Stack E-Commerce`

[Repository](https://github.com/vinitgirdhar/Sahaayak)

## Open Source

### AI Token Optimizer: Chrome Extension (20+ GitHub Stars)

Offline-capable Chrome extension that intercepts uploads of 5 file types (`.pdf` `.docx` `.xlsx` `.csv` `.md`) across 4 major AI platforms (Claude.ai, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity) and converts them locally in-browser into highly optimized Markdown before upload.

- Massive context window savings, lower API costs, and faster AI reasoning
- Removes platform-specific file size and file type friction
- All processing happens locally, with zero data leaving the browser

**Tech:** `JavaScript` `Browser Extension` `Local File Processing`
